# Contributing Guidelines

Thank you for your interest in contributing to this roadmap.

This repository is intended to remain **clear, structured, and skill-focused**.  
Contributions should improve accuracy, clarity, or usefulness without adding unnecessary complexity.

---

## What Contributions Are Welcome

- Corrections to factual inaccuracies
- Improvements to clarity or wording
- Additional resources that add clear value
- Structural improvements that improve readability

---

## What Will Not Be Accepted

- Tool dumps without context
- Low-effort additions or duplicates
- Marketing links or affiliate content
- Content encouraging illegal or unethical activity

---

## How to Contribute

1. Open an issue describing the proposed change  
2. Explain **why** the change improves the roadmap  
3. Keep proposals concise and focused  

Pull requests without context may be closed.

---

## Style Guidelines

- Use clear, professional language
- Avoid hype, buzzwords, or exaggerated claims
- Prioritize concepts over tools
- Keep additions minimal and purposeful

---

## Final Note

This roadmap reflects a specific learning philosophy.  
Not all suggestions will be accepted.

Quality and coherence matter more than volume.
